## Arm Catalyst Leicester

The Arm Catalyst service at the University of Leicester
provided a testbed for Arm-based HPC,
including both a CPU-only system funded by Arm,
and an Arm CPU + NVIDIA GPU-based testbed
funded as part of the ExCALIBUR Hardware and Enabling Software scheme.

### Gaining access

The facility is currently decommissioned,
so it is no possible to gain access.
